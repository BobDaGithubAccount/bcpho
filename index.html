Challenge 3

An analytic simulation of projectile motion with paramaters (X,Y) constraining the configuration space such that all trajectories conform to an intersection with (X,Y)
  • Grey: A trajectory that intersects (X,Y) with minimum initial velocity
  • Green: A trajectory that intersects (X,Y) with minimum initial velocity and a maximum launch angle (High ball)
  • Blue: A trajectory that intersects (X,Y) with minimum initial velocity and a minimum launch angle (Low ball)






Challenge 4

An analytic simulation comparing a trajectory of specified paramaters against the maximum ranged trajectory under variation w.r.t the launch angle
  • Red: A trajectory that provides maximum horizontal displacement given optimisation w.r.t launch angle
  • Blue: A trajectory created by provided paramaters
